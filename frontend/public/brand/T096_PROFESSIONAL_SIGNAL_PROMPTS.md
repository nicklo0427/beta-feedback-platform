# T096 Professional Signal Image Prompts

## Overview

This prompt pack defines the three homepage images used by the `可信試用訊號 / Trust signals` section.

The outputs map to the three tabs now used on the homepage:

- `professional-signal-not-shortcut.webp`
- `professional-signal-safety-first.webp`
- `professional-signal-platforms.webp`

This version allows people to appear in all three visuals. The goal is to keep the section trustworthy and product-like, while making it feel more human and more visually consistent with the homepage hero and product-flow artwork.

## Shared Art Direction

- Style: premium homepage brand illustration, bright, airy, modern, people-friendly.
- Visual family: must match the homepage hero and product-flow images.
- Tone: trustworthy, calm, structured, professional, but still warm and approachable.
- Composition: supporting-panel safe, 1536x1024 landscape crop.
- Palette: light-theme friendly, soft blue, cyan, pale lilac, clean neutrals.
- Product presence: abstract product UI, source cues, review checkpoints, device context, or workflow surfaces can appear, but they must stay secondary to the human scene.
- People: 2 to 3 people allowed, diverse, natural, not formal office stock-photo posing.
- Mood: this is a real testing-and-feedback workflow, not a hacky shortcut or manipulation tool.

### Global Negative Prompt

- no stock-photo corporate teamwork
- no literal dashboard screenshot
- no readable UI text
- no giant floating labels
- no app-store manipulation vibe
- no rating farm vibe
- no mutual review swapping energy
- no traffic-boosting tool look
- no customer-support center feel
- no stiff conference-room scene
- no semi-3D glossy character render
- no toy-like materials
- no dark hacker aesthetic

## Prompt 01 - Not a Shortcut / 不是捷徑

### Goal

Show that this is a real feedback-and-testing workflow, not a shortcut for exchanging reviews or inflating numbers.

### 中文 brief

生成一張首頁 `不是捷徑` 用的品牌插畫。畫面可以有人，但重點不是熱鬧的團隊感，而是「真的有人在看產品、看回饋、確認下一步」，不是互刷、換評、灌量。可以出現一位剛做完產品、想找真實建議的創作者，以及一位參與試用、提供真實反應的人，兩邊正在查看抽象化的回饋卡、狀態卡、來源提示或 decision checkpoint。整體要讓人感覺：這裡是認真收意見、整理下一步的地方，不是評論交換平台。

### English production prompt

Create a premium homepage brand illustration for a trust-signals section called "Not a Shortcut." People may appear in the scene. Show a real testing-and-feedback workflow, not review swapping or artificial growth. Include a creator who wants honest reactions on something they built, and a participant who is giving genuine feedback after trying it. Show abstract feedback cards, source cues, status markers, and review checkpoints, but keep them visual and secondary. The image should communicate that real people are checking, discussing, and organizing useful feedback, not exchanging ratings or boosting numbers. Style: bright, airy, modern brand illustration, light-theme friendly, soft blue/cyan/pale lilac palette, mature and trustworthy, aligned with the homepage hero and product-flow visuals. No readable text inside the artwork.

### Negative Prompt

no rating farm, no review exchange, no traffic boosting dashboard, no engagement pod look, no fake social proof scene, no sales-team celebration, no literal star-rating spam, no customer-service call center, no generic SaaS teamwork ad

### Crop / Framing Notes

- 1536x1024 landscape crop
- keep the human interaction readable at medium size
- leave enough calm space for the card layout
- make the workflow feel organized, not noisy

### Why this fits the section

This image helps users quickly feel that the platform is for structured, honest feedback and testing, not for gaming reviews or inflating metrics.

## Prompt 02 - Safety First / 安全優先

### Goal

Show that official channels, source visibility, and review judgment stay inside the workflow, with people still present in the scene.

### 中文 brief

生成一張首頁 `安全優先` 用的品牌插畫。畫面可以有人，而且要讓人覺得「有人真的在意來源、風險和分發方式」。可以出現創作者正在透過較正式的測試 / 分發方式準備邀請試用者，也可以出現參與者在自己的裝置上查看試用資訊、來源提示或風險提示。畫面中可加入抽象化的 official channel cue、source marker、risk cue、review checkpoint、status panel，但不要做成真實 dashboard 截圖。重點是：安全優先、官方管道優先、有人在看，不是隨便丟連結叫人裝。

### English production prompt

Create a premium homepage brand illustration for a trust-signals section called "Safety First." People may appear in the scene. Show a creator and participant in a calm, trustworthy workflow where official testing or distribution channels come first. Include abstract visual cues for official pathways, source labels, risk markers, and review checkpoints. The creator may be preparing a proper trial invitation, while the participant checks a device or install context with visible trust and safety signals. The image should feel like real people are using a careful, structured process where source, risk, and review judgment remain inside the workflow. Style: polished brand illustration, bright and airy, light-theme friendly, soft blue/cyan/pale lilac palette, aligned with the homepage hero and product-flow visuals. No readable text inside the artwork.

### Negative Prompt

no shady download vibe, no random APK sharing energy, no hacking scene, no dark cyber-security poster, no literal browser screenshot, no corporate compliance theater, no generic office meeting slide, no embedded UI text

### Crop / Framing Notes

- 1536x1024 landscape crop
- keep at least one human action clearly readable
- let safety and source cues register without overwhelming the people
- avoid compositions that feel too abstract or too technical

### Why this fits the section

This image makes the platform feel safer and more credible by showing that real people are paying attention to origin, review, and proper distribution flow.

## Prompt 03 - Supported Platforms / 支援平台

### Goal

Show that different devices and product types can be tried and coordinated in one human-centered workflow.

### 中文 brief

生成一張首頁 `支援平台` 用的品牌插畫。畫面可以有人，重點是讓人感覺這個平台可以支援不同裝置與不同型態的作品試用，例如網站、App、小遊戲。可以出現 2 到 3 位多樣化人物，在不同裝置情境中探索或參與試用，例如筆電、手機、平板或遊戲相關裝置提示，但不要變成硬體展示牆。畫面裡可以有抽象的任務卡、回饋提示、平台切換脈絡，讓人理解：不同平台也能在同一套流程裡被整理。整體要輕鬆、產品化、可信任，不要像商場賣場廣告。

### English production prompt

Create a premium homepage brand illustration for a trust-signals section called "Supported Platforms." People may appear in the scene. Show 2 to 3 diverse people exploring or participating in trials across different device contexts such as laptop, phone, tablet, or lightweight game-related cues. The image should communicate that websites, apps, and small games can all be coordinated in one calm, shared workflow. Include abstract task cards, device context, and feedback cues, but keep the composition people-centered rather than turning it into a hardware showcase. Style: polished brand illustration, bright, airy, approachable, light-theme friendly, soft blue/cyan/pale lilac palette, aligned with the homepage hero and product-flow visuals. No readable text inside the artwork.

### Negative Prompt

no crowded gadget wall, no electronics store poster, no esports event vibe, no literal operating-system UI screenshot, no embedded text, no chaotic multidevice marketing collage, no generic telecom ad energy

### Crop / Framing Notes

- 1536x1024 landscape crop
- keep people central and device variety secondary
- ensure platform diversity reads clearly at card size
- preserve a clean composition for mobile crop safety

### Why this fits the section

This image helps the platform feel broad enough for web, app, and game testing while still staying human, clear, and approachable.
